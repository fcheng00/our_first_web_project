Arena 1 Decks:


Basic Cycle

The Deck

  • Arrows: 3 elixir
  • Minions: 3 elixir
  • Musketeer: 4 elixir
  • Mini P.E.K.K.A.: 4 elixir
  • Spear goblins: 2 elixir
  • Wall breakers: 2 elixir
  • Knight: 3 elixir
  • Archers: 3 elixir

Average elixir: 3.0 elixir




Alternatives for some cards

Subs for Knight:

  1. Goblins: 2 elixir
  2. Prince: 5 elixir
  3. Hunter: 4 elixir

Subs for Arrows:

  1. Fireball: 4 elixir

Strategy

The overall idea of the deck is to cycle the Mini P.E.K.K.A. and the Musketeer, because the Mini P.E.K.K.A. can deal a lot of damage with the Musketeer behind it. Deploy the cheap troops for mostly for defending. Place pressure on the enemy with a Knight+Wall breakers charge or just Wall breakers. When defending, use Knight+Spear goblins+Musketeer or Mini P.E.K.K.A.+ Archers/Minions to destroy Giants and Baby dragons. In the deck, either use Mini P.E.K.K.A.+Minions, or include a Skeleton army to kill Prince.

When do you use it?

Use this basic but efficient deck in the ladder and 2v2s. In Triple Elixr mode, though, this cycle might not be ideal because it has way too many cheap cards. Since these cards have too little health, it is hard to make a push or defend against many powerful, costly cards at once. But you can still swarm and kill the enemy. In Balloon Festival, this deck might last, depending on the player. That is because there are many air defense troops. But, you'll need to controll the elixir used to defend against balloons since the enemy will also launch troops; probably to destroy yours.

Prince Beatdown deck

The deck

  • Prince: 5 elixir
  • Spear goblins: 2 elixir
  • Musketeer: 4 elixir
  • Goblin hut: 5 elixir
  • Archers: 3 elixir
  • Giant: 5 elixir
  • Minions: 3 elixir
  • Goblin barrel: 3 elixir

Average elixir: 3.8 elixir



Alternatives for some cards

Subs for Spear goblins:

  1. Baby dragon: 4 elixir
  2. Archers: 3 elixir
  3. Hunter: 4 elixir

Strategy

A beatdown deck contains of tanks and supports. In this deck, the Prince and the Giant are the tanks; strong, damaging, and sturdy. Then, the other cards stay behind the tanks to support while staying alive. The Goblin Barrel is for extra damage on towers of distractions so that the tank can reach the tower. If the enemy has a prince or Giant, then throw down the Goblin hut to distract the enemy troops. DO NOT use the tanks in this deck for defending. That is because if the enemy has a cycle going, you'll have an effective counter only once with the Prince. It costs too much elixir to use defending.

When do you use it?

This deck is great in Triple elixir because of the effective high elixir cards. It might not be as effective in the ladder due to the attack only style cards. If the enemy uses a cycle deck, you might be out of luck. 2v2s might be a problem, since your enemy might use a cycle type deck, resulting in this deck completly failing. Also, there are two opponents in 2v2, so you might be playing two diffrent deck types.

Prince cycle

The Deck

  • Prince: 5 elixir
  • Skeleton army: 3 elixir
  • Goblins: 2 elixir
  • Spear goblins: 2 elixir
  • Wall breakers: 2 elixir
  • Goblin barrel: 3 elixir
  • Baby dragon: 4 elixir
  • Minions: 3 elixir

Average elixir: 3.0



Alternatives for some cards

Subs for Spear goblins:

  1. Archers: 3 elixir
  2. Musketeer: 4 elixir

Subs for Prince:

  1. Hog rider: 4 elixir
  2. Knight: 3 elixir
  3. NOT RECOMMENDED Giant: 5 elixir

Strategy

This cycle contains a Prince, which can deal a huge load of damage when it is backed by a Baby dragon. During times without the Prince in the cycle, you can launch a Goblin barrel at the tower and then spawn the Wall breakers. Throw down the barrel first to get the enemy to attack the goblins. Then, the Wall breakers will have a good chance to launch their bomb, which can be devestating. Use the other troops to lay a shield in front of the Prince or throw them behind the Prince. Most likely, if the enemy has a Prince or Mini P.E.K.K.A., this will lure it out. Then, the Skeleton army will get to work. Note that when you're playing a first charge, don't use more than eight elixir if possible, unless you're using a Baby dragon with the prince. A swarm can be killed by Arrows, and then a Skeleton army kills the Prince.

When do you use it?

This deck is useful in most battles. The ladder, 2v2s, and Triple elixir is no problem for this deck, as it cycles the Prince and Baby dragon. That results in an easy win even with not as skilled players. However, in Balloon festivals, this deck is not as effective because it does not have many air defense cards.
