Minecraft

Basic Weapons

In Minecraft, a sword deals a lot of damage and is the most used. There are different kinds of swords. Some people have bow and arrows. They are good ranged weapons to kill creepers.

Armor

Armor is a way to give yourself more hitpoints. There are classic armor and other ones like a turtle shell. Some things you wear don't give extra hp, like a pumpkin helmet. They prevent Endermen from attacking you.

Getting armor

Usually, you get armor by crafting or collecting it. Diamond armor is especially hard to get.
